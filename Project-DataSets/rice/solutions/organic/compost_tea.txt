COMPOST TEA – Organic Pest & Disease Management Solution
Crop: Rice (Also applicable for Maize and Vegetables)
Category: Organic / Eco-Friendly Treatment

------------------------------------------------------------

1. What is Compost Tea?

Compost tea is a liquid organic fertilizer and plant health booster 
made by steeping mature compost in water. It contains beneficial 
microorganisms, nutrients, and natural compounds that help suppress 
harmful pathogens and improve plant immunity.

------------------------------------------------------------

2. Purpose in Pestora System

Compost tea is recommended when:

• Pest damage is mild to moderate
• Confidence score is between 60% – 85%
• Leaf symptoms are early-stage infection
• Farmer prefers organic-first approach

It helps in strengthening plant resistance rather than directly 
killing pests.

------------------------------------------------------------

3. Benefits

• Enhances natural plant immunity
• Improves soil microbial activity
• Suppresses fungal diseases (e.g., Leaf Blast, Brown Spot)
• Environmentally safe
• Non-toxic to humans and animals
• Improves overall crop growth

------------------------------------------------------------

4. Preparation Method

Ingredients:
• 1 kg well-decomposed compost
• 10 liters clean water
• 1 tablespoon jaggery (optional – boosts microbial growth)

Steps:
1. Place compost in a cloth bag.
2. Submerge in a bucket of water.
3. Stir thoroughly.
4. Let it steep for 24–48 hours.
5. Filter before spraying.

------------------------------------------------------------

5. Application Method

• Spray directly on affected leaves.
• Apply during early morning or late evening.
• Repeat every 7–10 days.
• Avoid spraying during heavy rain.

------------------------------------------------------------

6. Effectiveness Level

Best suited for:
• Partially damaged leaves
• Early fungal infections
• Preventive care

Not sufficient for:
• Severe infestation (>90% damage)
• Advanced fungal spread

------------------------------------------------------------

7. When to Escalate

If symptoms worsen after 2–3 applications:
→ Move to chemical treatment (government-approved only)
→ Follow Pestora confidence-based recommendation logic

------------------------------------------------------------

Safety Note:
Compost tea should be prepared hygienically.
Use fresh compost to avoid harmful bacteria contamination.

------------------------------------------------------------

Pestora Recommendation Logic:
Organic solutions are always suggested first.
Chemical pesticides are recommended only when:
Confidence Score ≥ 90%
Severity Level = Fully Damaged
